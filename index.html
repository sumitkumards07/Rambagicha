About Us

Rambagicha Guest House in Tirumala is a peaceful stay option for devotees. Located conveniently near the main temple, it offers a serene environment for spiritual rejuvenation. Our guest house is dedicated to providing a comfortable and well-maintained atmosphere where pilgrims can find rest and peace.

Why Choose Us?

We pride ourselves on being a family-friendly stay with helpful support assistance. Our commitment to cleanliness and a peaceful environment ensures that your focus remains on your spiritual journey. With modern essentials like free WIFI and hot water, we bridge the gap between tradition and contemporary comfort.
